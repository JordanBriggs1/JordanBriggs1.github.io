Importance and Culture of Manual

One very important feature at Manual is the "M" in the Lobby. it's quite disrespectful to step
on the "M" at Manual, because it's Manual's original logo, and it represents Manual's community as a whole.

Manual Highschool also has their own Military training, for those who wish to be in the U.S. Military.
Hell, they even have trophies, as well as models of their planes and/or jets!
